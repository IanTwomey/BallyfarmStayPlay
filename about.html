Meet our team


John Neal John has spent a large part of his life travelling the world with particular emphasis on living ashrams in India and Tibet and has only recently returned to Ireland to share the benefits of his experiences. In his time abroad he's absorbed the best of these Eastern Philosophies and fuses it with our ancient shamanic practices and wisdom.



Anne Hegarty A seasoned horse riding instructor has won many local trophies in show jumping. With a life time experience around horses she has an almost innate ability to put horses at ease, with a very patient relaxed personality she loves to pass on her knowledge to the public.

Michael Jacob A Cork born and bred native and a seasoned chef who's worked in the kitchens of some of the finest establishments in London he's returned to his native cork with a view to putting a more local slant to the dishes he's learned in London. He's looking forward to teaching residents his own personal Cork favourites Tribe and Drisheen, and Smoked Fermented Salmon, all sourced locally.
