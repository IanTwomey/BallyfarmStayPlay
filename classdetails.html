Scheduled Activities


Meditation Enjoy our open air meditations, we’re sure you’ll find the tranquil countryside adds to your meditation experience. There's nothing like meditating in the fresh air to help connect with mother nature. Meditation has been shown to have numerous health benefits, it aids dealing with stress, insomnia, depression and general fatigue.



Yoga - John is eager to share his knowledge of Eastern Yogic traditions. Enjoy outdoor yoga (weather permitting) yoga as its meant to be. I’m sure you’ll find yoga on the farm much different, horse yoga is a highly relaxing experience.

If practiced with consistency, Yoga improves flexibility, builds strength and develops control and endurance in the entire body. It puts emphasis on alignment, breathing, developing a strong core, and improving coordination and balance.

Horse Riding Our world class horse riding instructor Anne Hegarty will put your through your paces, truly the best way to enjoy this pristine countryside.

Mountain Hiking For the more adventurous John Neal will take you to a trip to the majestic Galtymore mountains nearby.
