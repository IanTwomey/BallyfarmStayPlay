Our location: Ballyfarm StayPlay is located in the heart of Cork in the South of Ireland. As well as providing overnight guest accommodation we are a working Organic farm with sheep, Irish Moiled Cattle and Irish Draught Horses. Cork Airport and Rosslare Ferry Port are the nearest points of entry into the country. If using a Smart Phone App please enter either Eircode P67 AW72 or Ballyfarm, Cork.From Mitchelstown take the R665 signposted for Mallow. At the first roundabout turn a right. Follow this narrow country lane for approx 1 km and our entrance will be on the left hand side. There is a sign at the gate. You can see the house from the road. Please note that we live in a very rural location you won't be able to search for Ballygarrane on Google you will have to search with Google coordinates.!
