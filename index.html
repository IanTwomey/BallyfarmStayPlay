Welcome to Ballyfarm StayPlay- For those who want an action packed stay on farm.

Most people love the countryside but when they get there they decide they've nothing to do. We ensure you won't get bored while enjoying the beauty of the countryside. We organise activities for people both on the farm and with local hiking groups. Country walk feel like you've walked in to a painting or mountain hiking for the more adventurous.

We're looking for the distinguished traveller who really wants to experience the local culture. There are people who want to drive around the countryside and tick all the boxes but our visitors like to stay and experience the local environment and what it has to offer. We're also looking for that self reliant traveller who wants to add value to his or herself while travelling so we offer cookery, meditation, yoga and horse riding classes.
